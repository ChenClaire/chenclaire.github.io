  Case study 1: Multi-Lanuage and CC

User Needs & Business Needs

User Needs: Some of Grovo's enterprise customers have overseas employees who are non-English speakers and work out side of U.S. These oversears employees want to learn Grovo lessons in their own languages.

Business Needs: As Grovo's partnership continues to expand, we need to support additional languages for potential global customers.

Goal & Scope

After talking to Engineering, Sales, and Customer Success teams, as well as translation agency, we decided, for the first step of this project, we will support:

1. translation of text content in each lesson into Spanish, French, German and Portuguese;

2. CC of video content in each lesson in Spanish, French, German and Portuguese.
